Once logged in you can click on the "Edit" button on the bottom of each summary, this will take you to the CMS where you can edit the summary.

Once logged in navigate to the Contribute page and click on the "Add new event" or "Add new person" button, this will take you to the CMS where you can add new events or people.

Once the changes are submitted they undergo a review process, this is to ensure that the changes are correct and that they follow the guidelines. Once the changes are approved they will be visible on the website.
